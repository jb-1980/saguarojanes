Our Mission

We seek to follow the admonition of Jesus to his disciples as recorded in Matthew 14:16, "They do not need to go away. You give them something to eat." We will do this by actively working with organizations in our community to gather food to distribute to those who are hungry.

Our Story

Saguaro Jane's begin in the 1940s when a small group of women decided to assist the families of those who had loved ones who went off to war. Since then, our organization has grown to a group of FIND THE NUMBER of men and women volunteers who work tirelessly to provide food to our community.

True to our desire to help, our organization is composed of volunteers. No one is paid, and all donations go to helping out the hungry and needy through our various programs. Thanks to the generous donations of others, we are able to make a difference in our community and give something to eat to the hungry.
